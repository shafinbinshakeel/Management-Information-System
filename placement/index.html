Scholarship

The Scholarship is provided to meritorious students applying to the Study in India Programme. It covers the tuition fees, accommodation (room and boarding), and canteen costs. The scholarship amount is paid up to USD 3500 per year, per student and any charges over or above it have to be paid by the student. The allocation of the Study in India Scholarship is done on the basis of the PRAGATII exam, a standardized online exam for students seeking scholarships under the programme. The PRAGATII Exam scores serve as the criteria to shortlist the students for the scholarship.
